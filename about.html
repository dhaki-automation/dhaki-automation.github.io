WHO WE ARE

Our name, “Dhaki” (ذَكِي) derived from the Arabic word for “smart,” reflects our commitment to intelligence and innovation in every project we undertake. At Dhaki Automation is where innovation meets elegance in the world of smart automation. Based in Dubai, we specialize in creating bespoke automation solutions for homes, offices, and hotels, all designed to enhance your lifestyle with unmatched convenience, security, and luxury.


At Dhaki Automation, we understand that technology should not only make life easier but also enrich the spaces where you live, work, and relax. That’s why we offer a wide range of automation services, from lighting and climate control to security systems and entertainment solutions, all fully integrated into a seamless, user-friendly experience. What sets us apart is our excellent customer service and dedication to user's privacy and security, ensuring that our cutting-edge technology always respects your personal space and data.


Our team of experts is passionate about delivering high-quality, customized solutions that align with your unique needs and aesthetic preferences. Whether you’re looking to transform your home into a smart sanctuary, modernize your office environment, or elevate the guest experience in your hotel, Dhaki Automation is here to make it happen.
